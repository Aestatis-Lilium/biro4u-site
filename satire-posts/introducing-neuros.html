How It Works

neurOS™ uses advanced neurotechnology to monitor your brain activity and convert it into a digital currency called neuroCOIN™. The more neural activity you generate, the more neuroCOIN™ you earn. It's that simple!

But we didn't stop there! Everyone has moments they wish they could relive, and now you can! neurOS™ is reinventing the meaning of "POV" with Impulse NFTs™. ImpulseNFTs™ are unique digital assets that represent specific moments in time as they were experienced by you. From the underappreciated wisdom your parents gave you, to the first time seeing your child's face, these experiences can be perfectly preserved through neurOS™ integration with ImpulseNFTs™. These NFTs can be traded on our exclusive marketplace, neurOSIS™, allowing you to profit from your most unique moments and brilliant ideas.

NeuroCHAIN™, built on revolutionary web 3.0 technology, makes it possible to offer neuroCOIN™ and Impulse NFTs™ while a dedicated community lovingly referred to as, "Neurotix", is constantly improving the experiences of these offerings.
